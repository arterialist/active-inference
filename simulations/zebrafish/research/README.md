# Zebrafish Research Sources

Source discovery date: 2026-05-13.

Goal: collect a practical, traceable inventory of public zebrafish resources
needed to implement a larval zebrafish active-inference simulation later.

The corpus focuses on:

- Connectome and synapse-level EM data.
- Whole-brain calcium, voltage, and activity mapping data.
- Locomotion, tail kinematics, behavior repertoires, and body mechanics.
- Brain/body anatomy, atlases, ontologies, and genome resources.
- Programmatic access paths and legal/licensing notes.

The most simulation-relevant anchors are Fish1/CAVE, FishExplorer/mapzebrain,
Z-Brain, Janelia whole-brain functional recordings, DANDI zebrafish datasets,
the Marques locomotor repertoire data, ZFAP/FishNet body models, and ZFIN/ZFA
anatomy resources.

Use `candidate_literature.md` for recent PubMed hits that were found but not
yet curated into `source_manifest.json`.
